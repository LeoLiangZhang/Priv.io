How to Register

  1. On the priv.io home page, click "Registration."

  2. Enter your desired username, password, and email.

  3. The “type of storage” dropdown menu has three options: Amazon S3, WebDAV, and Dropbox. Click the one you want connected to your priv.io account.

  4. In the next two boxes, put the Access Key and the Secret Key to the above account. Please click here if you want more information on S3 keys.

  5. Click the “Register” button.

Heads up! Please wait while system setting up your account. This might take some time.

How to add Friends

  1. You will need to know your friend's username and he/she will need to know yours, so make sure you both have this information.

  2. Sign in to your priv.io page and click on the "Friends" link on the upper left of the page.

  3. Enter your friend's username in the "Friend ID."

  4. In the next box, you need to decide how information will be shared. You can choose one or more sharing policies.

  5. Click on the "Add!" button. Your friend's username will appear in the Friend ID column, with a status of "pending".

  6. Once your friend accepts your friend request, the "pending" status will be removed.

S3 Keys

  • One of the storage types supported by Priv.io is Amazon Simple Storage Service (S3).

  • To use this storage type, you will need an access key and a secret key. The access key is a form of username, while the secret key is a password. Together, these keys allow photos and information stored in your cloud to be displayed on your priv.io page.

  • If you need keys for S3 storage, email Liang Zhang at liang@ccs.neu.edu.

Limiting Audience

  1. For each post or album you make, you can decide which of your friends should be able to see it.

  2. You can type the sharing policy or policies (i.e. friend or family), the username or usernames of specific friends (i.e. chloe or liang), or type “@@” which shares the content with all your friends. If you write multiple tags, they should be separated by a single space (i.e. friend family chloe)

How to Post a Message or Photo

  1. After signing in, your home page will display two boxes. One says “Your message” and the other one says “Your sharing policy.”

  2. Type what you want to say in the first box or click the photo button to add a picture.

  3. Decide which of your friends you want to see your message. Click here for information on sharing policies.
Heads up! It may take several minutes for your friends to see your message or photo.

How to Post a Photo Album

  1. On your home page, click on the link on your sidebar that says “Photo Album.”

  2. Click the blue button above where it says “Album.”

  3. In the boxes that say “Album name” and “Your sharing policy,” add the title of the album and the sharing policy respectively. Click here for information on sharing policies.

  4. Select the photos you want to upload from your computer and drag them to the box that says “Drop photos here.” Note: you will need to drag all the photos at once or else they will not all load.


      5. Heads up! It may take several minutes for your friends to see your photo album.
